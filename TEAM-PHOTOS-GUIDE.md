## üìê Photo Specifications

### Technical Requirements:
- **Format**: JPG (JPEG)
- **Dimensions**: 400x500px (portrait) or 400x400px (square)

- **Color Mode**: RGB

### Quality Requirements:
- **Focus**: Sharp, clear face
- **Lighting**: Well-lit, no harsh shadows
- **Background**: Solid color (white, grey, or navy blue)
- **Framing**: Head and shoulders visible
- **Expression**: Professional, friendly, confident
- **Attire**: Business formal or business casual

## üì∑ How to Take Professional Photos

### Option 1: Professional Photographer
**Best for**: Highest quality results
- Hire a professional photographer
- Request business headshots
- Specify dimensions and background
- Get digital files in high resolution

### Option 2: DIY Professional Photos
**Best for**: Budget-friendly option

**Equipment Needed**:
- Smartphone with good camera (iPhone, Samsung, etc.)
- Good lighting (natural window light or ring light)
- Plain background (wall or backdrop)
- Tripod or stable surface

**Steps**:
1. **Setup Location**
   - Find well-lit area (near window is best)
   - Use plain background (white/grey wall)
   - Avoid busy or cluttered backgrounds

2. **Camera Settings**
   - Use portrait mode if available
   - Ensure good focus on face
   - Use highest resolution setting
   - Take multiple shots

3. **Positioning**
   - Subject stands 3-4 feet from background
   - Camera at eye level
   - Subject looks at camera
   - Shoulders slightly angled (not straight on)

4. **Lighting**
   - Natural light from window (best)
   - Face the light source
   - Avoid harsh overhead lights
   - No strong shadows on face

5. **Take Photos**
   - Take 10-20 shots per person
   - Try different angles
   - Vary expressions slightly
   - Check focus and lighting

## üñºÔ∏è Photo Editing & Preparation

### Step 1: Select Best Photos
- Choose photos with:
  - Clear, sharp focus
  - Good lighting
  - Natural expression
  - Professional appearance

### Step 2: Crop & Resize
Use free online tools:

**Canva** (https://canva.com):
1. Create custom size: 400x500px
2. Upload photo
3. Crop to show head and shoulders
4. Center the face
5. Download as JPG

**Photopea** (https://photopea.com):
1. Open photo
2. Image > Canvas Size > 400x500px
3. Crop tool to adjust framing
4. File > Export As > JPG
5. Quality: 80-90%

### Step 3: Optimize File Size
Use compression tools:

**TinyJPG** (https://tinyjpg.com):
1. Upload your photo
2. Download compressed version
3. Should be under 200KB

**Compressor.io** (https://compressor.io):
1. Upload photo
2. Choose "Lossy" compression
3. Download optimized file

### Step 4: Rename Files
Rename to exact filenames:
- ibrahim-kamilu.jpg
- aminu-adamu.jpg
- khaled-nasir.jpg
- ismail-kamilu.jpg
- ibrahim-musa.jpg

**Important**: Use lowercase, exact spelling, no spaces

### Step 5: Upload to Website
Place all 5 photos in: `images/team/` folder

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Photos Not Showing?
1. Check filenames match exactly
2. Verify photos are in `images/team/` folder
3. Clear browser cache (Ctrl+F5)
4. Check file format is JPG
5. Verify file permissions

### Photos Look Stretched?
1. Check original dimensions
2. Maintain 4:5 aspect ratio
3. Use object-fit: cover in CSS (already set)

### File Size Too Large?
1. Use TinyJPG to compress
2. Reduce dimensions if needed
3. Lower JPG quality to 80%
