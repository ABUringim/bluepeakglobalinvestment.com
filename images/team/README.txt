PHOTO TIPS:
- Use a professional photographer if possible
- Ensure good lighting (natural or studio)
- Plain background (white, grey, or navy blue)
- Subject should be centered
- Face should be clearly visible
- Professional business attire
- Friendly, confident expression
- High resolution (at least 400x500px)

HOW TO PREPARE PHOTOS:
1. Take or collect professional photos
2. Crop to show head and shoulders
3. Resize to 400x500px or 400x400px
4. Optimize file size (use TinyJPG.com if needed)
5. Save as JPG format
6. Rename to exact filenames above
7. Place in this folder
